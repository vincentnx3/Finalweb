services

Food Service

Room Service is available 24/7. Wether you like dinner for breakfast or breakfast for dinner at the Houston Hotel we will make it. You can also enjoy a delicious meal at one of our resturants in the hotel. Restaurants are open till 11pm.

Refreshment

We serve all kind of refreshments, from juices to alcoholic drinks. We have an open bar down in the lobby and in the restaurants. Must show prove of ID.

Housekeeping

Keeping each room clean after every use is our high priority. We make sure every room is cleaned and bed sheets are changed after every booking.

Room Security

We have round the clock security and state of the art security cameras all around the hotel and inside. We require for each guest staying in the hotel to have parking permit placed on the dashboard of each car.
